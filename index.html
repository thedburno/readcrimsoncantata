Crimson Cantata

A commander with a vision. A living weapon disguised as nobility. A scholar bound by a pact. A flower feeding on hatred. An exile who devours without hesitation. A man willing to sacrifice everything.
They should never have met, but so they did. Their reasons are their own, yet they stand side by side. In a world where power should dictate worth and blood fuel ambition, they carve their path through civilization and savagery alike. After all, what is the difference between man and monster?
They are not heroes, nor are they martys. But if the world must burn for their ideals, then so be it.
